- **Six AI Difficulty Levels**: Sophisticated AI opponent with 6 difficulty settings
- **Game Features**:
  - Multiple piece themes and board designs
  - Time controls
  - Move history
  - Undo/redo functionality
  - Sound settings
  - Flip board option
- **Published App**: Available on both Google Play and App Store
- **Monetization**: Includes ad integration (banner and interstitial ads)
- **Modern Architecture**: Uses Flutter with GetX, Provider, and other modern packages
- **Game Logic**: Implements proper chess rules, castling, en passant, promotion, etc.
